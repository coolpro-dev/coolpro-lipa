What is the difference between general and chemical cleaning?

General cleaning covers filters, coils, drain line, and exterior wash — ideal every 3–6 months. Chemical cleaning is a deeper flush when there is mold, odor, weak cooling, or heavy buildup.

Do you service split-type, window, and inverter AC?

Yes. We work on split-type, window, floor-standing, and inverter units for homes and small commercial sites in Batangas.

My aircon is not cooling — can you repair it?

Yes. We troubleshoot not cooling, leaks, noise, and units that won't start. Parts and labor are quoted on-site before work proceeds.

Do you install new aircon units?

Yes. We provide split-type installation and relocation labor in Lipa and Batangas. The AC unit is typically purchased separately unless we arrange otherwise.

How often should I clean my aircon in the Philippines?

For most home units in humid Batangas weather, general cleaning every 3–6 months helps airflow and cooling. Chemical cleaning is only when buildup or odor persists.

Do you offer refrigerant top-up?

Yes, after a pressure check and leak inspection when applicable. We use R410A or R32 depending on your unit label.
