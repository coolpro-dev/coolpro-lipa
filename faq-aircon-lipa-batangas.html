Aircon Service FAQ

Common questions about cleaning, repair, booking, and service areas in Lipa & Batangas.

What areas do you serve?

We dispatch from Lipa City to Tanauan, Santo Tomas, Malvar, Mataasnakahoy, Talisay, Batangas City, Rosario, San Jose, Ibaan, Cuenca, Balete, Padre Garcia, and nearby Batangas towns.

How do I book aircon service?

Message us on Messenger or WhatsApp with your AC type, HP, issue, and barangay. We confirm scope and schedule before your visit. You can also call 0948 512 1132.

What is the difference between general and chemical cleaning?

General cleaning covers filters, coils, drain line, and exterior wash — ideal every 3–6 months. Chemical cleaning is a deeper flush when there is mold, odor, weak cooling, or heavy buildup.

Do you service split-type, window, and inverter AC?

Yes. We work on split-type, window, floor-standing, and inverter units for homes and small commercial sites in Batangas.

Do you service Tanauan, Santo Tomas, and Malvar?

Yes. We regularly visit Tanauan, Santo Tomas, Malvar, and other towns near Lipa. Send your location on Messenger so we can confirm schedule.

My aircon is not cooling — can you repair it?

Yes. We troubleshoot not cooling, leaks, noise, and units that won't start. Parts and labor are quoted on-site before work proceeds.

Do you install new aircon units?

Yes. We provide split-type installation and relocation labor in Lipa and Batangas. The AC unit is typically purchased separately unless we arrange otherwise.

How often should I clean my aircon in the Philippines?

For most home units in humid Batangas weather, general cleaning every 3–6 months helps airflow and cooling. Chemical cleaning is only when buildup or odor persists.

Do you offer refrigerant top-up?

Yes, after a pressure check and leak inspection when applicable. We use R410A or R32 depending on your unit label.

How do I get a quote?

Message us on Messenger with your service needed, AC type, HP, and location. We confirm pricing and scope before dispatch — no surprise charges for agreed work.

How much does aircon cleaning cost in Lipa or Batangas?

Pricing depends on AC type, HP, condition, and location. Message CoolPro on Messenger with those details — we confirm a quote before your visit with no surprise charges for agreed work.

Why is my aircon leaking water indoors?

Common causes are a clogged drain line, dirty filters, or improper installation slope. We inspect the drain pan and line during cleaning or repair and clear blockages when needed.

Is chemical cleaning safe for inverter aircon?

Yes, when done correctly on the indoor coil and drain system. We use chemical cleaning only when general cleaning is not enough — not on every routine visit.

How long does a general aircon cleaning take?

Most single split-type home units take about 45–90 minutes depending on buildup and access. We confirm timing when you book.

Can you service aircon in condos and apartments?

Yes, for homes and small units in Lipa, Tanauan, Santo Tomas, and nearby Batangas towns. Tell us building rules or time windows when you book.

Do you relocate existing split-type aircon units?

Yes, we provide relocation labor — uninstall, move indoor/outdoor units, and reinstall with new routing when needed. The AC unit stays yours; we quote labor on-site.

What aircon brands do you work on?

We service common residential brands for split-type, window, and floor-standing units. Send your unit label photo on Messenger if you are unsure.

What should I prepare before the technician arrives?

Clear access to the indoor unit, outdoor unit, and electrical outlet. Have someone home to open the unit area. Pets secured in another room help the visit go faster.

Why does my aircon smell bad when turned on?

Odor often comes from mold or bacteria on the evaporator or drain pan. General cleaning may help; persistent smell may need chemical cleaning.

Can you top up freon without cleaning first?

Sometimes, but weak cooling can be caused by dirty coils or a leak. We check pressure and inspect for leaks first — topping up a leaking system is temporary.
